How ArthSahay Works

End-to-end flow from onboarding to UPI payout

01

Onboard via WhatsApp (2 min)

Name · Zone · Platform · Avg daily earnings → Policy active instantly

↓
02

AI Risk Profiling

Zone baseline built from 90-day historical data. Seasonal modifier applied. Trust score initialised at 50.

Zone Threshold = μ(zone, 90d) + k × σ(zone)
↓
03

Dynamic Weekly Premium

AI-calculated each week. ₹15–₹40 range based on zone risk + season + trust tier.

Premium = Base(2.5%) × ZoneRisk × Seasonal × (1 − TrustDiscount)
↓
04

Disruption Detected

Agentic AI continuously monitors rain, AQI, traffic, platform demand. Multi-signal fusion determines level.

Rain(L3) + AQI(L2) → fused → L3 payout
↓
05

AI Validation (4 checks)

✅ GPS in disruption zone ✅ Order volume drop confirmed ✅ No GPS spoof detected ✅ No prior claim today
↓
06

Payout Calculated & Sent

Payout = LostHours × (DailyEarnings ÷ 8) × LevelPct%

UPI credit within 24 hours. WhatsApp confirmation sent.
